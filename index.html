How to Detect & Avoid Scammers:

  • Check the Profile: Scammers often use recently created accounts with very few friends or photos.
  • Urgency Tactics: Be wary of messages claiming your account will be deleted or asking for "urgent help" with money.
  • Suspicious Links: Never click on links that ask for your login credentials outside of the official Facebook app.
  • Identity Spoofing: Scammers may impersonate your friends. Always verify unusual requests through a different communication channel.
